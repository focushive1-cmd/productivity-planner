Productivity Tips
Systems that actually work.
01
Time Blocking
Divide your day into focused blocks for specific tasks. Batch similar work to protect deep concentration time.
02
The Pomodoro Method
Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a 20-minute rest to sustain energy.
03
Eat the Frog
Tackle your hardest task first every morning — before email or social media. Everything else feels easy after.
04
The 2-Minute Rule
If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately. Small tasks pile into overwhelming backlogs if deferred.
05
Weekly Review
Every Sunday, spend 15 minutes reviewing wins, identifying improvements, and setting clear intentions for next week.
06
Single-Tasking
Multitasking splits attention and reduces quality. One task, one objective, full focus beats fragmented effort always.
07
Energy Management
Schedule demanding tasks at your personal energy peak. Reserve low-energy periods for email and routine admin.
08
The Night Capture
Before sleeping, write tomorrow's top 3 tasks. Your mind processes them overnight — you wake with clear direction.
